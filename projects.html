  • AIRY Balloons

    Technologies Used: HTML5, CSS3/SCSS, JS, Node.js, Netlify, Git, GitHub, npm, Figma

    AIRY is an e‑commerce site for selling and delivering balloons, built as an SPA/landing page with additional functionality (product catalog, product detail page, shopping cart, checkout, etc.). The site is deployed on Netlify and uses Serverless functions to handle orders. When an order is placed, a message with the cart and form data is automatically sent to Telegram.

    The homepage (landing) is implemented as a single‑page application, while the additional pages (catalog, product page, cart, etc.) are loaded separately. This approach helps organize the site’s information and functionality more effectively.

    • Product catalog with category filtering
    • Individual product pages with detailed descriptions
